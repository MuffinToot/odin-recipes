Egg Fried Rice

A basic Egg Fried Rice recipe you can pair with any stir-fry or saucy dishes! In less than 15 minutes, anyone can easily make this recipe.


  • Course: Main Course, Side Dish
  • Cuisine: Asian, Chinese
  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Cooked rice
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 2 Tbsp Garlic, chopped
  • 4 Tbsp Light soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Oyster sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Sesame oil
  • 5 Spring onions, sliced thinly
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Wisk the eggs in a small bowl and set aside. Cut the spring onions into thin slices.
  2. Heat a large wok/pan over medium-high heat. Drizzle 1 Tbsp of vegetable oil, pour the whisked eggs in. Let the eggs almost set for a few seconds, stir and break into small pieces.
  3. Push the eggs to one side, drizzle a bit more oil and stir-fry the chopped garlic for a few seconds.
  4. Place the cooked rice in the wok, pour the soy sauce and oyster sauce in. Stir well to combine and seperate the rice using a spatula.
  5. Stir occasionally for 1-2 minutes, drizzle sesame oil and sprinkle salt to taste.
  6. Turn off the heat, add chopped spring onions and toss well.
  7. Transfer to serving plate and serve immediately.

You can serve 2-3 people as a main meal, or 4-5 people as a side dish. Enjoy!
